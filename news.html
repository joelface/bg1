October 31, 2023

Genie+ support restored

You can once again book/modify Genie+ Lightning Lanes in BG1!

March 20, 2023

Back button/gesture support added

At long last, you can now use your browser's back button or swipe gesture to return to the previous screen! You'll see a back arrow in the upper left corner of any screen where it's possible to use the back button.

Plans tab added to home screen (Genie+)

You can now see your plans for your entire trip instead of just today's reservations.
